# Use Cases: Retail

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Retail industry.

*   **Inventory & Restocking:** Generate purchase/transfer orders in vendor or warehouse portals based on stock levels.
*   **POS & Catalog Updates:** Bulk-update product info (prices, descriptions) on store systems.
*   **Customer Returns Processing:** Enter refund or exchange details, update stock levels and issue refunds.
