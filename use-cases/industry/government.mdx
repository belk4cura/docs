# Use Cases: Government

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Government sector.

*   **Benefits Applications (Social Services):** Pull applicant data, fill online portals for unemployment, food assistance, etc.
*   **Permit/License Requests:** Auto-populate multi-step forms for building permits, business licenses.
*   **Immigration Filings:** Gather personal info, attach docs, fill visa or residency forms in government portals.
