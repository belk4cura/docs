# Use Cases: Healthcare (Medical)

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Healthcare and Medical industry.

*   **Patient Intake & Registration:** Auto-fill EHR forms with patient demographics, insurance details, and consent forms.
*   **Insurance Claims Processing:** Populate claim forms (CMS-1500, etc.) with diagnoses/procedures; submit to payers.
*   **Appointment Scheduling & Pre-Authorization:** Secure approvals from insurers and schedule specialist referrals in web portals.
