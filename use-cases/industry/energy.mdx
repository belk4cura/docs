# Use Cases: Energy & Utilities

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Energy and Utilities sector.

*   **Customer Account Setup:** Transfer service application data (address, usage, rates) into billing systems.
*   **Meter Reading Entry:** Bulk-input meter readings from spreadsheets or emails into CIS for billing.
*   **Equipment/Regulatory Reporting:** Update compliance forms (maintenance data, emissions) in agency websites.
