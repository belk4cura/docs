# Use Cases: Legal

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Legal industry.

*   **Court E-Filing:** Enter case details, upload documents/motions into judicial portals.
*   **Contract Automation (NDAs, Service Agreements):** Populate standardized templates from questionnaires.
*   **Compliance Reporting (SEC, Regulatory):** Transfer financial/operational data into regulatory web forms.
