# Use Cases: Logistics

This page outlines common use cases for Curalabs within the Logistics industry.

*   **Freight & Customs Docs:** Auto-complete shipping manifests, bills of lading, and customs declarations.
*   **Shipment Tracking & Booking:** Enter booking data into carrier portals, update internal TMS with tracking info.
*   **Inventory Reconciliation:** OCR supplier invoices, match POs/receipts, update ERP systems.
