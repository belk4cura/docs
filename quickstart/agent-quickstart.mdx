    **Troubleshooting: Can't See Chat UI?**

    If after refreshing, you cannot see the chat UI, you may need to toggle the 'Show Agent' button in the extension pop-up. This involves a few clicks:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="2a. Click Extension Icon">
        Click the puzzle piece icon to open the Chrome Extensions menu.
        <div class="small-step-frame">
          <Frame>
            ![Chrome Extension Toolbar Icon](/images/steps/chrome-ext-btn.png)
          </Frame>
        </div>
      </Step>
      <Step title="2b. Pin Extension (Optional)">
        Find the 'Cura Digital Assistant' and click the pin icon next to it. This keeps the Cura icon visible in your toolbar for easier access.
        <div class="small-step-frame">
          <Frame>
            ![Pin Extension Icon](/images/steps/pin.png) 
          </Frame>
        </div>
      </Step>
      <Step title="2c. Toggle 'Show Agent'">
         Click the pinned Cura icon in your toolbar to open its popup menu. Ensure the 'Show Agent' toggle is enabled.
        <div class="small-step-frame">
          <Frame>
            ![Extension Popup Menu](/images/steps/popup.png)
          </Frame>
        </div>
      </Step>
    </Steps> 
  </Step>
